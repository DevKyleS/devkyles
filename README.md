## Skills & Experience

With extensive experience across cloud infrastructure, site reliability engineering, and DevSecOps, I specialize in:
- **Compliance & Security Maturity** — Led organizations through SOC II Type II certification and currently advancing GovRAMP compliance posture
- **Securing the SDLC** — Maturing CI/CD pipelines with integrated linting, build quality gates, SAST/DAST, and dependency scanning—shifting security left without sacrificing developer velocity
- **Reliability at Scale** — Designing systems that maintain high availability and performance under regulatory scrutiny
- **Infrastructure as Code** — Building reproducible, auditable, and maintainable cloud environments that pass compliance reviews
- **Platform Engineering** — Creating developer-friendly tools that abstract complexity without compromising security or auditability
- **Data-Driven Engineering** — Leveraging observability, metrics, and SLO/SLI frameworks to drive decisions on reliability, capacity, and risk
- **AI Tooling Engineering** — Designing and shipping LLM-powered automation tools that reduce operational toil and surface insights across engineering workflows
- **Agentic Automation** — Building agentic AI pipelines that automate cross-platform workflows (Jira, Confluence, ticketing) and deliver actionable intelligence to stakeholders

I deliver secure, compliant infrastructure that enables operational excellence and supports teams in achieving their mission.
